Birds Spotted



The Bird Spotting map below allows you to double click on the map and any birds spotted in the area will be marked with the common name of the bird, the number spotted and the location. The information is provided through the Cornell Lab of Ornithology eBird API. For additional information on how you can help add to the database of birds spotted in your area you can go to eBird

If you want additional information on the birds spotted, suggested sites include All About Birds and Audubon.

Please note that TakWare LLC is not associated with the Cornell Lab of Ornithology or Audubon.
